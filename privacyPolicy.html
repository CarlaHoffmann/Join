Furthermore, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority. You can contact us at any time regarding these and other questions about data protection.

4. Data Collection on This Website

Cookies

Our website uses so-called "cookies." Cookies are small data packets that do not harm your device. They are stored on your device either temporarily for the duration of a session (session cookies) or permanently (permanent cookies). Session cookies are automatically deleted at the end of your visit. Permanent cookies remain stored on your device until you delete them yourself or an automatic deletion by your web browser occurs.

If you send us inquiries via the contact form, your details from the inquiry form, including the contact data you provided, will be stored for the purpose of processing the inquiry and for follow-up questions. We do not share this data without your consent.
